About US

Promptcart is an AI-powered grocery delivery platform designed to offer a seamless, convenient, and prompt shopping experience. We make everyday grocery shopping easier for everyone by delivering fresh, affordable priced products straight to your doorstep in a matter of minutes with our user-friendly app.

Our Mission

Revolutionize the grocery shopping experience by emerging as the most dependable and preferred platform, offering a seamless, convenient, and accessible to all the people of Kerala, making their lives easier every day.

Our Vision

To become the most preferred super app, renowned for solving a multitude of customer concerns and making life easier for millions of users across the globe.
